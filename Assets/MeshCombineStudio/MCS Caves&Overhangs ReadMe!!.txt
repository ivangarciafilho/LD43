This is a free extension for MeshCombineStudio 2 and therefore reques it:
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/tools/modeling/mesh-combine-studio-2-101956

After importing MeshCombineStudio 2 the instructions will be visible in the Example Cave scene in the ReadMe!! GameObject.

For complete documentation click on the `Documentation` button in the MeshCombineStudio prefab.

If you have any issues or questions post it on the MCS Caves & Overhangs Unity forum thread:
